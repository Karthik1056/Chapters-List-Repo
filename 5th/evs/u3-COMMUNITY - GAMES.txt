The community has given rise to games.

To relax and spend free time, people have created games as a means of recreation.

Games help build relationships among individuals in a community and provide an opportunity for elders and youngsters to play together.

Games are activities formed for entertainment as well as physical exercise.

They increase harmony within the community by encouraging everyone to play and enjoy together.

Physical and mental health develop when games, yoga, and physical exercises are practiced regularly according to need.

They make the body strong, help maintain body weight, and improve physical appearance by giving the body a good structure.

Regular physical activity increases self-confidence and helps in the beneficial use of free time.

As games provide entertainment and happiness, they keep us active, healthy, and cheerful.
